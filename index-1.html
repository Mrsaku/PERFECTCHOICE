About Our Store

Beauty and tradition, shelved together

Perfect Choice Cosmetics & Bangles is a family-run store in Kusumgram, Purba Bardhaman, built by owner Sakibullah Bora around a simple idea — that genuine cosmetics and beautiful accessories shouldn't be hard to find or afford.

Under one roof, we bring together everyday makeup essentials, skincare and haircare staples, fragrances, and a colourful world of bangles, earrings, necklaces and hair accessories. Every shelf is stocked with trusted, authentic brands and refreshed regularly, so there's always something new to discover.
